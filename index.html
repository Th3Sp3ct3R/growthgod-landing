Capabilities

Everything you need to stay ahead of the market

Four intelligence modules that work together to give you an unfair view of your competitive landscape.

01

Competitive Intelligence

Monitor competitor accounts across all major platforms. Know what they post, when they post, and what actually resonates with their audiences.

  • Real-time follower and engagement tracking
  • Content cadence and format analysis
  • Share-of-voice breakdowns by keyword
  • Side-by-side performance comparisons
02

Audience Analytics

Go beyond vanity metrics. Understand the demographic and psychographic makeup of any public audience — yours or a competitor's.

  • Age, location, and interest segmentation
  • Audience overlap and affinity mapping
  • Follower authenticity and quality scoring
  • Growth velocity and churn signals
03

Content Performance Benchmarking

Compare your content output against industry benchmarks. Find the gaps and opportunities your team is missing every week.

  • Industry-specific engagement baselines
  • Format performance by platform and niche
  • Optimal posting time recommendations
  • Trending topic and hashtag intelligence
04

Market Research

Turn social data into strategic insight. Surface emerging trends before they peak and validate assumptions with real behavioral signals.

  • Trend forecasting from leading social signals
  • Sentiment analysis at scale
  • Influencer landscape mapping
  • Campaign performance attribution
Process

Up and running in under ten minutes

01

Connect your accounts

Add the accounts you want to track — yours and your competitors'. We handle authentication and start pulling data immediately. No scripts, no scraping to set up.

02

We index the data

GrowthGod continuously collects engagement, audience, and content data across all connected accounts. Historical data is available from day one for accounts with public histories.

03

Surface the insights

Your dashboard surfaces what matters: where you are ahead, where you are falling behind, and where the next opportunity is. Exportable reports available at any time.
